Location

The museum is located at Standgaten 18, 5013 Bergen, Norway. You can take public transport to the nearest stop which is Byparken and from there its about a five minute walk. We also have guest parking for a hourly fee of $1.50.

Accessibility

The museum has wheelchair accessibility ramps. It also has audio guides and braille display signs for the visually impaired.

Food and drink

There is a café attached to the museum where you can get light lunches, soft drinks, coffee, snacks and more.
